The Why

Highly successful retailers in the world have all adopted the strategy of multi-channel marketing. They have a strong online presence with engaging content to increase sale both online and offline. One of the most powerful tool they have utilized is social media. As research has shown, 84% of young consumers were influenced by social media when making a purchasing decision. Unfortunately, having a successful social media marketing strategy is extremely difficult for the following reasons:

Creating meaningful and actionable content

Developing and maintaining a highly followed social media page

Costing of social media marketing is constantly increasing

The Solution

FOCUZ.io is a platform that features the following core instruments in an effort to solve the social media marketing problems faced by the businesses:

Quest System - Increase Exposure

Community Input on Quest - More Exposure

Budget Friendly - Low Cost but High Engagement

Leads Generation - Drive Quality Traffic

Earn Cryptocurrency Easily - Unique Reward for Effort with Cause

How it works

  • Business posts a Quest

    Business post Quest – Exposure through the Focuz.io user base.

  • Promoter completes and submits the Challenge

    Exposure when Promoters completing the Challenges

  • Community validates submission

    Exposure when the whole community is looking into these submission to validate

  • Promoter and Community members get FCZ token as reward for effort

    Promoter uses FCZ token reward from Quest to buy voucher

  • Promoter makes purchases with FCZ token on focuz.io

    Promoter purchases with voucher on Business’ website or store – Gain exposure for potential upsell in fiat currencies (dollars)

The Quest System

Quest is a promotional-purposed challenge posted by business. A Quest can be in the form of image, audio, video, or even reviews. As an example, a restaurant may post up a Quest to invite people to come in their restaurant to try out a new dish. Upon approval of Quest completion, the Questee will receive FCZ tokens as a reward.

e-Commerce

Blockchain enabled decentralized decision making allows the whole community to help validating the submission from a Questee. This prevents bias from any individual party. Most importantly, it creates a significant higher exposure as more people will see the business and its brand. Validators of the winning side will also be receiving FCZ tokens as a reward. Winning side is either APPROVED or REJECT of the submission.

Free-to-use

There is no need to worry about budget because businesses get to post 1 free Quest per month as long as they list 50 vouchers for sale for FCZ token.

Community validation

Vouchers will need to be redeemed at the business’ physical or web store. This new traffic of consumers are visiting with buying intents for the possibility of upsell in fiat money.

Earn Cryptocurrency Easily

Earning FCZ token requires no technological expertise nor does it require a powerful computer. It can be earned as an individual or as a social event. Most importantly, all these effort will be helpful in promoting local businesses and support our economy. Now, that’s a cause worth having fun for!
