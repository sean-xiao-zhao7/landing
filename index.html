The Why

According many researches, the consensus for the top marketing challenges businesses are:

Not having enough traffic and leads

Lack of money

Lack of money to do marketing campaigns and even if there are sufficient money, it’s hard to figure out what to do that will generate the most returns

How it works

the Focuz cycle

  • Business posts a Challenge

    Business post Challenge – Exposure through the Focuz.io user base.

  • Promoter completes and submits the Challenge

    Exposure when Promoters completing the Challenges

  • Community validates submission

    Exposure when the whole community is looking into these submission to validate

  • Promoter and Community members get GAGE as reward for effort

    Promoter uses Focuz reward from Challenge to buy e-gift card

  • Promoter makes purchases with Focuz on focuz.io

    Promoter purchases with e-gift card on Business’ website or store – Gain exposure for potential upsell in fiat currencies (dollars)

The Challenge System

Free-to-use

There is no need to worry about budget because businesses get to post 1 free challenge per month as long as they list a gift card for sale with a minimum of 5 quantities! Listing products for sale is always free.

Community validation

Upon completion of challenges, the community will validate promoter’s submission to ensure it meets all, which means a ton of online exposure for businesses.

You don’t need a powerful computer or being tech-savvy to earn coins. Anyone from all age can get Focuz.io through challenges.
